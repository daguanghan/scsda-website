# Launch Decision Packet

## Decision

The current review decision is whether the staging site at
`https://ultraclaw.space/` is acceptable as the formal English SCSDA website.

No registrar, DNS, or paid-service action is authorised by this packet.

## What The Site Should Do

- Present SCSDA as a nonprofit applied research academy.
- Explain the Academy's background, mission, leadership, research themes,
  selected projects, outputs, archive note, and contact route.
- Present Dr Daguang Han's role in SCSDA through formal leadership content.
- Use period-labelled figures from the 2019-2023 platform-construction record.
- Use real SCSDA public images and project visuals.

## What The Site Must Not Do

- Read like a strategy memo or self-explaining personal positioning page.
- Present SCSDA as a government website or official authority.
- Publish confidential, military, surveillance, public-security, or sensitive
  technology-transfer wording.
- Overstate current institutional scale.
- Claim that one person alone completed every institutional output.
