# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

The primary users are international academic readers, research collaborators,
industry partners, and professional contacts who need a concise and credible
English account of Smart Cities and Sustainable Development Academy.

## Product Purpose

This website presents SCSDA as a formal nonprofit applied research academy. It
should read like an institutional website: clear background, research themes,
platform structure, selected public projects, outputs, leadership, and contact
routes.

The site can support Dr Daguang Han's broader academic profile by making his
role in SCSDA visible, but it must not explain itself as a strategy page,
personal advertising page, or persuasion memo.

## Brand Personality

Careful, institutional, research-led, and public-facing. The tone should be
formal enough for university and research audiences while avoiding official
government, confidential, military, surveillance, or exaggerated platform
language.

## Anti-references

Do not make the site look like a generic SaaS landing page, personal vanity
portfolio, government authority site, public-security platform, or decorative
dark-tech brochure. Avoid purple-blue gradients, glassmorphism, nested
card-heavy layouts, fake claims, over-rounded cards, vague innovation slogans,
and repeated small uppercase labels.

## Design Principles

1. SCSDA is the subject of the site.
2. Dr Han's role appears through formal leadership content, not through
   explanatory meta-copy.
3. Every strong claim should be public-facing, non-confidential, and bounded by
   period or source where needed.
4. Research credibility beats visual excitement.
5. Historical Chinese-site material can be reused after filtering for
   international readability, current relevance, and risk.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast and readable mobile layouts. Motion should be
restrained and never required for comprehension. Navigation should remain
plain, keyboard-accessible, and understandable to readers scanning quickly.
